Curriculum

8 Topics. Complete Coverage.

Every concept across all four resources maps to these eight core topics.

TOPIC 01

Field Theory for PDN

Energy in the dielectric, not the copper. Why power is delivered between planes, not from them.

TOPIC 02

Target Impedance

f_max = 0.5/t_rise. Z_target = V_noise / I_inst. Setting the design constraint before component selection.

TOPIC 03

Via Inductance

The 60% spacing rule. Why partial inductance is meaningless. Loop inductance of power-ground pairs.

TOPIC 04

Capacitor Mounting

End vs side vias. Polarity cancellation. Same-side mounting. Alternating orientation around BGAs.

TOPIC 05

Decoupling by Speed

Energy circles. Low-speed intentional inductance. High-speed plane dominance. OPD and ODC at gigabit speeds.

TOPIC 06

Plane Inductance

Per-square measurement. IC placement and field geometry. Why 4-layer planes are nearly useless above 100 MHz.

TOPIC 07

Board Stackup

Copper pour technique. 10× inductance improvement. Near-surface planes and the 40% cap improvement.

TOPIC 08

EMI & Compliance

How poor PDN creates EMI. Stitching via rules. Layer transition field coupling. The EMI test failure pattern.
