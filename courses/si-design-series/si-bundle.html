Curriculum

8 Topics. Complete Coverage.

Every concept across all four resources maps to these eight core SI topics.

TOPIC 01

EM Field Propagation

Signals as electromagnetic fields in the dielectric. Rise time vs clock frequency. The signal wavefront. Why BW = 0.35/tr.

TOPIC 02

Return Path

Return current follows path of least inductance at high frequency. No routing over gaps. Zone partitioning vs split planes.

TOPIC 03

Characteristic Impedance

Geometric control: trace width, dielectric height, εᵣ. Reflection coefficient. Vias as discontinuities. Back-drilling.

TOPIC 04

Reflections & Termination

Critical length: l = (tr/6) × vp. Series, parallel, Thevenin strategies. Topology impact on termination choice.

TOPIC 05

Crosstalk

Capacitive + inductive coupling. NEXT vs FEXT. 3W rule. Orthogonal layer routing. Differential pair discipline.

TOPIC 06

Ground Bounce

ΔV = L × di/dt. Simultaneous switching noise. Decoupling strategy. Where PDN and SI design converge.

TOPIC 07

SI Routing Rules

Every signal layer needs a reference. Return via at every transition. Orthogonal layers. Differential length matching.

TOPIC 08

Interface-Specific Design

DDR fly-by topology. SerDes AC coupling and back-drilling. RF/ADC boundary isolation. USB differential requirements.
